# Email copy (tone reference)

**Source of truth:** [`../tone-and-content-strategy.md`](../tone-and-content-strategy.md)

Transactional templates (e.g. `welcome-early-access.html`) should follow that doc: approachable, clear, helpful, trustworthy; sound like a **collector**, not a corporation; **direct**, not wordy; **helpful**, not pushy; avoid financial-heavy language and hype; prefer **“your collection”** over “portfolio” unless context calls for it; use the **Dex** language system naturally (“Dex it,” “Snap. Dex. Done.”).

Sign-off in templates: **The DexIt team** (product name per strategy). Legal/footer may still say **Dexitcards** where that matches your site.

**Avoid em dashes** in user-facing copy; use commas, periods, or parentheses instead.
